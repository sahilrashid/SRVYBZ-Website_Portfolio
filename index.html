A Little Bit About SR VYBZ

At the beginning of 2020, the world had just been introduced to the COVID-19 pandemic. Lockdowns and business closures started to emerge as the months progressed, in order to halt the spread of the virus.

At that moment, I had the idea of learning to cut my own hair, this very idea has grown into SR VYBZ, a local at-home barbershop where quality and experience are the first priority. Taking advantage of the opportunity that the pandemic had provided, I have been able to create a space where individuals are able to feel comfortable and confident in their appearance. Haircuts and beard lineups are just the tip of the iceberg when it comes to what SR VYBZ has to offer.
